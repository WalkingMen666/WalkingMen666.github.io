Game Instructions

Keyboard Controls:

  • Arrow Left (←): Move left
  • Arrow Right (→): Move right
  • Arrow Up (↑): Rotate piece
  • Arrow Down (↓): Soft drop
  • Space: Hard drop

Score Calculation:

  • 1 line cleared = 1 point
  • 2 lines cleared = 4 points
  • 3 lines cleared = 9 points
  • 4 lines cleared = 16 points
